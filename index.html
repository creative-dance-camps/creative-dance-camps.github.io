What to expect

Gender neutral creative dance classes that build foundational dance technique skills from a playful approach, working towards creating, improvising, and performing. Expect dance games, movement inspired by books and art, folk dances from around the world, and more! Each camp has 4 classes (avoiding year-long dance commitments) and culminates in an “In-formance,” an informal performance where families can come watch the final class and see how their dancer has grown!

Meet Alli

Alli is passionate about providing high quality dance classes for kids to learn and explore movement. Although she is a Utah native, she most recently lived in Pittsburgh, PA where she taught creative dance classes for both children and adults in the community. After moving to Springville this year, a big basement space seemed to beg for dance classes to start up again!

12 Years of Experience:
Alli has been a dance teacher for 12 years, teaching all ages and styles of dance.
Dance Education Degree:
Alli graduated with a Bachelor’s of Art degree in K-12 Dance Education from Brigham Young University with a minor in Math Education. Before that she trained competitively at eMotion Dance Studio in Sandy, Utah.
Performance Experience:
While at BYU Alli danced with the International Folk Dance Ensemble as well as the contemporary group dancEnsemble. In Pittsburgh, Alli choreographed and danced in multiple dance films with her performing group Moonrise Dance Company.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the cost? Enrollment for one student is $35 for a camp that has four classes. Payment is due upon enrollment (Venmo) or in person at the first class (cash or check).
  • Where is the studio space? Classes are located in Alli’s basement living room near Spring Creek Park. Address will be provided upon enrollment. There is a separate entrance to the basement studio space on the side of the house.
  • What should my child wear? Any clothes comfortable for moving and grooving! They should dance barefoot or in tennis shoes.
  • What should my child bring? Just a water bottle!
  • Am I allowed to stay and watch? Drop-off is preferred, but there is space for you to stay and watch quietly in the back if your dancer needs that support.
  • What if we need to miss a class? No refunds or makeups will be provided if your dancer needs to miss a class. However, if Alli needs to cancel a class for unforeseen circumstances, she will provide a make up class on the week after the camp ends.
